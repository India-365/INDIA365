International Flower Festival

Date: 1st – 31st May 2024

Location:Governor’s residence, Gangtok

Description:This festival brings together a range of fun-filled activities, the beauty of flowers and some decent knowledge about plantations. Many species of orchids, gladioli, cacti, roses, trees, ferns, alpine plants, creepers, climbers, are displayed during the show. A range of regional delicacies for your taste buds are also there. For the ones who love adventure, there is river rafting. Another interesting feature of the Flower festival in Sikkim is the Yak Safari. Apart from this, lectures and seminars about indigenous plants are conducted.

Modes

By Air: Fly to Bagdogra Airport: Book a flight to Bagdogra Airport (IXB) from major cities like Delhi, Kolkata, Mumbai, or other cities with regular flights.

Travel from Bagdogra to Gangtok: Hire a pre-paid taxi or shared cab from Bagdogra Airport to Gangtok. The journey is scenic and passes through beautiful landscapes.

By Train: There is no direct train to Gangtok, but you can reach the nearest railway station and then proceed by road.

Reach New Jalpaiguri (NJP) or Siliguri Railway Station: New Jalpaiguri (NJP) and Siliguri are major railway stations in West Bengal. From NJP or Siliguri, proceed to Gangtok by road (taxi or shared cab). The road journey takes around 4-5 hours.

By Road: Gangtok is well-connected by road to major cities in Sikkim and neighboring states.
