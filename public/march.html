Chapchar Kut

Date: march 1st

Location:Mizoram

Description:This is a cultural festival with dance, song and other exciting celebrations by the locals. This festival marks the end of winter and the beginning of the beloved spring. This is also the time when the fields are at the post-harvest stage, where the farmers slash and burn the agricultural remains in the fields. This festival takes place on the first Friday of the month of March, every year.

Modes

By Air: By Air: Lengpui Airport: This is the primary airport serving Aizawl, the capital city of Mizoram. Lengpui Airport is well-connected to major cities like Kolkata, Guwahati, Imphal, and other cities in Northeast India. You can take a direct flight to Lengpui Airport from these cities and then travel to Aizawl by road.

By Train: Silchar Railway Station: The nearest major railway station to Mizoram is Silchar in Assam. Silchar is connected to cities like Guwahati and Kolkata via train. From Silchar, you can take a taxi or bus to reach Aizawl, the capital of Mizoram. Keep in mind that the railway network in Northeast India is limited compared to other regions.

By Road: From Guwahati: One of the common routes to reach Mizoram by road is from Guwahati, Assam. You can take a bus or hire a taxi from Guwahati to Aizawl, which takes approximately 6-8 hours depending on road conditions.

From Silchar: Another route is from Silchar, Assam, which is relatively closer to Mizoram. You can travel by road from Silchar to Aizawl, which takes around 5-6 hours. From Agartala: If you are traveling from Tripura, you can also reach Mizoram by road via National Highway 44. The journey from Agartala to Aizawl takes about 24-26 hours by road.

Local Transport: Taxis and Shared Jeeps: In Mizoram, taxis and shared jeeps are common modes of transport for local travel within the state. You can hire a taxi or join a shared jeep to reach specific destinations within Aizawl or other parts of Mizoram. Rental Cars and Motorbikes: Renting a car or motorbike is another option for exploring Mizoram independently. Several rental agencies offer vehicles for hire in Aizawl.
