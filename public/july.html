Karsha-Gustor Festival: A unique Buddhist Festival

Date:

Description:The largest and the most important monastery in Zanskar, the Karsha Monastery celebrates this festival annually to recognize the victory of good over evil. From a Black Hat Dance performance to a Masked Dance performance by the monks, this festivity is full of uniqueness. Apart from the joyful music and dance, one also gets to witness the vibrant colours of the region during Karsha Gustor. Needless to say, this is one of the Indian festivals in July which are so worth attending!

Visiting this destination during this festival is one of the best things to do in Ladakh.

Date:When: 15th to 16st July 2024

Where: Zanskar, Ladakh

Festival Highlights: Black hat dance performance

Modes

By Air: Zanskar does not have any airports yet. You can either fly into Srinagar or Leh and then carry on towards Zanskar Valley. Kushok Bakula Airport in Leh is the nearest domestic airport.

By Railway: Zanskar or any other parts of Ladakh is not connected with railway lines, so there is no direct train to Zanskar Valley. However, you can travel by train up to Jammu Tawi. Jammu Tawi railway station is around 720 km far from Zanskar valley.
