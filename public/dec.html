Rann Utsav


Description:Kutch, one of the largest salt deserts in the world, celebrates the Rann Mahotsav by showcasing & demonstrating the authentic Gujarati culture. The mesmerizing cultural extravaganza of India includes colorful & vibrant folk performances, enthralling adventure sports, and ethnic gigs. All of this, in addition to the traditional cuisine & the impeccable hospitality, makes Rann Mahotsav one of the most awe-inspiring festivals celebrated in India.


Date: 1st November - 20th February

Where: Kutch, Gujarat

Festival Highlights: Musical performances, dance shows, camel riding, horse riding, photography, archery, and paramotoring
